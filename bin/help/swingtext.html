Swing

The Swing tracks provide efficient ways to create swing. There are two types of swing tracks:
  1. The global swing track. (to the right of the "LZ" track)
    The global swing track applies swing to all tracks in a block.
  2. Swing subtracks. (to the left of note text)
    Swing subtracks applies swing to normal tracks, and overrides the global swing track for that track. Right click a track and enable "Swing text" to edit swing of individual tracks.

Swing text format: xxt, where 

    
xx = Swing weight. 1 = lowest value, 99 = highest value.
t  = Whether to gradually change tempo to the next swing value. (Press 't' to switch)

  • To remove a swing entry, press DEL.
  • To stop swing in the middle of a block, add "1" (or any number) to the first beat in a bar, and remove all other swing values from that bar.
  • To disable all swing in a block (can be used to quickly compare swing vs. non-swing), unselect the check box below "Swing" in the upper left part of the editor.
