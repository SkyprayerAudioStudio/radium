Swing

The Swing tracks provice efficients way to add swing to a block. The global swing track applies swing to all tracks in a block, and the swing sub tracks applies swing to individual tracks (overriding the global swing track). Right click on a track header and enable "Swing text" to edit swing of individual tracks.

Swing text format: xxt, where 

xx = swing weight. 1 = lowest value, 99 = highest value.
t = Whether to gradually change tempo to the next swing value. (Press 't' to switch)

  • To remove a swing entry, press DEL.
  • To stop swing in the middle of a block, add "1" (or any number) to the first beat in a bar.
  • To disable all swing in a block (can be used to quickly compare swing vs. non-swing), unselect the check box below "Swing" in the upper left part of the editor.
