Swing

The Swing tracks provice efficients way to add swing to a block. The global swing track applies swing to all tracks in a block, and the swing sub tracks applies swing to individual tracks. Right click on a track header and enable "Swing text" to edit swing of individual tracks.

Swing text format: xxt, where 

xx = swing weight. 1 = lowest value, 99 = highest value.
t = Whether to gradually change tempo to the swing value.

Example of a shuffle swing: 

"
3
-
2
-
3
-
2
-
3
-
2
-
3
-
2
"

To remove a swing entry, press DEL.

To disable all swing in a block (can be used to quickly compare swing vs. non-swing), unselect the check box below where it's written "Swing" in the upper left part of the editor.
