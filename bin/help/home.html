Main GUI

  1. Menus
    Use the left alt key to show/hide menus and start/stop navigitating the menus. You can also stop navigating the menus by pressing Esc. Hold shift to quicker navigate up and down when using the up and down arrow keys.
  2. Editor
    • FX text
    • Chance text
    • Swing text
    • Velocity text
  3. Blocklist
    The blocklist shows a list of all available blocks.
    • Click a block to change current block in the editor.
    • Doubleclick a block to add it to the playlist.
    • Shift + rightclick a block to delete it.
  4. Playlist
    The playlist is a textual representation of the current track in the sequencer. The playlist does not display more information than the sequencer, but it can be more convenient to use, especially if there is only one track in the sequencer.
  5. Mixer
  6. Mixer strip for current instrument
    Shows a mixer strip for the current instrument. The current instrument is shown in the instrument widget. Input MIDI signals are also sent to this instrument.

Mouse

  • To finetune movement on Linux and Windows: Press the Ctrl key.
  • To finetune movement on macOS: Press the Cmd key.

  • Limit movement to the vertical direction: Press Shift key.
  • Limit movement to the horizontal direction on Linux and Windows: Press the Left windows (Meta) key.
  • Limit movement to the horizontal direction on macOS: Press the Left Alt key.

  • To delete something: Press the Shift key and click Right mouse button.

  • To select/unselect something on Linux and Windows: Press the Ctrl key and click Left mouse button.
  • To select/unselect something on macOS: Press the Cmd key and click Left mouse button.

  • To zoom in or out: Press the Ctrl key and use the Mouse scroll wheel.

  • To scroll vertically: Use the Mouse scroll wheel.
  • To scroll horizontally: Press the Shift key and use the Mouse scroll wheel.

Terms

  • Block - What is known as a "pattern" in trackers.
  • Velocity - The volume of a single note.
